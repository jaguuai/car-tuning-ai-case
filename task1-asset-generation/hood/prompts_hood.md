### Notes / Iteration Log
- Reused the proven background/lighting/no-logo formula from the
  previous three categories without modification.
- Chose a top-down slight angle instead of a 3/4 or straight-on angle,
  since a hood is viewed and judged mostly from above in real use, and
  its key design features (vents, bulges, crease lines) are not visible
  from a side or frontal elevation.
- Unlike the Front Bumper case, the hood's front-to-back axis does not
  raise the same "direction ambiguity" problem, since the geometry
  itself (narrower front edge vs. wider rear edge, vent placement)
  gives the model a clear orientation cue without needing explicit
  left/right language.
- All three outputs matched on the first attempt: consistent white
  background, consistent framing/angle, no surface badge or lettering.
